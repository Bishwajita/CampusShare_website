Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell or give away an item?

To sell or give away an item, go to the "Upload Item" page, fill in the details about your item (e.g., category, description, price), and submit your listing.

How do I contact a seller?

Once you find an item you're interested in, you can contact the seller directly through the contact details they have provided (email or phone number).

What payment methods can I use?

Payment methods are arranged directly between the buyer and seller. We recommend secure methods like bank transfers or using PayPal.

How do I delete or edit my listing?

To delete or edit your listing, go to your profile page and select the listing you want to manage. You can edit the details or remove it completely.
